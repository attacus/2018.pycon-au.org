Thanks to generous donations by other attendees, we have some additional financial assistance budget!

When buying a ticket for PyCon AU, attendees have the option of paying a bit extra to help someone else make it to PyCon AU. Thanks to a number of generous people, we are now able to open financial assistance applications again.

Unforunately for this second round, it's too close to the conference to organise visas and long-distance travel. We will be granting financial assistance requests only for free tickets and travel assistance from within Australia and nearby countries. We still expect you to tell us a bit about why you need assistance, and we'll be assessing each case individually.

Please fill out this financial assistance application form to request a free ticket or travel and accommodation expenses and we'll get back to you within a few days.

If you have the funds, don't forget that tickets for PyCon AU are still on sale! You still have the option of adding a bit extra to our financial assistance pool to help someone else.❤️
